title: "Enabling Natural Language Voice-to-Code Dictation in Talon Voice with LLMs"
duration: "2026.02-2026.03"
excerpt: "Computer science students with physical disabilities struggle to interface with a keyboard and mouse, and thus write code. Voice-to-code technologies, such as Talon Voice, help alleviate this burden. However, to our knowledge, no existing voice-to-code software supports natural language commands, which further cognitively burdens the user. To that end, in a class project, we proposed and implemented an extension to Talon Voice that enables natural language dictation via an LLM-based interpreter."
